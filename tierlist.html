Technology Knowledge Tier List 2022

Just to give you an idea of how familiar I am with some techologies, here is a tier list of them. In case you do not know, tier lists are a way of sorting things in a hierarcical order based some quality. S is the highest and F is lowest.

Here the list is ordered by evaluating how familiar the techology is to me:

  • S = I pretty much use these daily
  • A = I have used these in multiple projects recently
  • B = I know how to use these, but dont use them regularly
  • C = I have used these relatively much on some university course for example, but have not used them outside of that situation
  • D = I have used these a bit on some university course for example, but have not used them outside of that situation
  • F = I have tested these out somewhat, and know what they are, but do not really know them all that well
